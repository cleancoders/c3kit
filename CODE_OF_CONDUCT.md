# Code of Conduct

This project follows the
[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
By participating in any c3kit community space — issues, pull requests,
discussions, or any other channel — you agree to abide by it.

## Reporting

Reports of unacceptable behavior may be sent privately to
**gina@cleancoders.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly, and the privacy of reporters will be respected.

## Enforcement

Enforcement consequences follow the
[Contributor Covenant Enforcement Guidelines](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/#enforcement-guidelines),
ranging from a private written notice up to a permanent ban for sustained or
severe violations.

For answers to common questions, see the
[Contributor Covenant FAQ](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/faq).
